# User Stories - Veil Shine

This document outlines user stories for the Veil Shine platform, written in a Gherkin-style format to clearly define functionality from the perspective of different user personas.

## 1. Core Anonymity & Account Management

### Story: Anonymous Account Creation
**As a** new user,
**I want to** create an account using only a unique handle,
**So that I can** use the platform without revealing any personal information.

### Story: Secure Account Recovery
**As a** user,
**I want to** generate and store a client-side passphrase during account creation,
**So that I can** recover my account if I forget my handle, without the platform storing my recovery phrase.

### Story: View Anonymous Profile
**As a** user,
**I want to** see a basic profile with my chosen handle and an anonymous avatar,
**So that I can** have a consistent identity within the platform without revealing personal details.

## 2. Content Creation & Sharing

### Story: Create Anonymous Text Post
**As a** Confider,
**I want to** write and publish a text post up to 500 characters,
**So that I can** share my thoughts and experiences anonymously.

### Story: Categorize Post
**As a** Confider,
**I want to** select a category for my post from a predefined list,
**So that my post can** be easily discovered by others interested in that topic.

### Story: Comment on a Post
**As an** Explorer or Contributor,
**I want to** write an anonymous comment on a post,
**So that I can** engage in discussions and share my perspective without revealing my identity.

### Story: Reply to a Comment
**As an** Explorer or Contributor,
**I want to** reply to an existing comment,
**So that I can** participate in threaded conversations.

### Story: React to Content
**As a** user,
**I want to** apply an anonymous reaction (e.g., upvote, emoji) to a post or comment,
**So that I can** express my feelings or agreement without typing a full response.

### Story: Report Inappropriate Content
**As a** user,
**I want to** report a post or comment that violates community guidelines,
**So that I can** help maintain a safe and respectful environment.

## 3. Content Discovery & Feed

### Story: View Personalized Anonymous Feed
**As an** Explorer,
**I want to** see a feed of anonymous posts tailored to my interests,
**So that I can** discover content relevant to me without my personal data being used.

### Story: Browse Trending Content
**As an** Explorer,
**I want to** view a section of currently trending anonymous posts,
**So that I can** stay updated on popular discussions.

### Story: Explore by Category
**As an** Explorer,
**I want to** browse posts within specific categories,
**So that I can** find content on topics I am particularly interested in.

### Story: Search for Content
**As an** Explorer,
**I want to** search for posts using keywords,
**So that I can** find specific topics or discussions.

## 4. AI Integration

### Story: AI Content Moderation
**As a** platform administrator,
**I want the** AI to automatically flag and, if necessary, remove content that violates community guidelines,
**So that I can** maintain a safe environment with minimal manual intervention.

### Story: AI Sentiment Analysis for Posts
**As a** Confider,
**I want to** see an AI-generated sentiment analysis of my post (e.g., positive, neutral, negative) after publishing,
**So that I can** understand the general emotional tone of my contribution.

### Story: AI-Generated Post Suggestions (Future)
**As a** Confider,
**I want to** receive AI-generated suggestions for post categories or tags based on my content,
**So that I can** improve the discoverability of my posts.

## 5. Moderation & Safety

### Story: View Moderation Queue
**As a** moderator,
**I want to** view a queue of AI-flagged and user-reported content,
**So that I can** review and take appropriate action on potentially harmful content.

### Story: Ban Abusive Users Anonymously
**As a** platform administrator,
**I want to** ban users who repeatedly violate community guidelines,
**So that I can** remove disruptive elements while preserving their anonymity from other users.
