# Product Roadmap - Veil Shine

This document outlines the phased product roadmap for Veil Shine, detailing key milestones and features across different versions. The roadmap is designed to be agile, allowing for adjustments based on user feedback, market trends, and technological advancements.

## 1. Roadmap Overview

Our journey for Veil Shine will unfold in distinct phases, starting with a focused MVP to validate core assumptions, followed by iterative enhancements to build a comprehensive and engaging anonymous social platform.

## 2. Phases and Key Milestones

### Phase 0: Minimum Viable Product (MVP) - (Current Focus)

**Goal**: Launch a foundational anonymous social platform to validate core anonymity, content creation, and AI moderation.

**Key Features**:
*   **Anonymous Account Creation**: Handle-based registration, no PII.
*   **Client-Side Passphrase Recovery**: User-controlled account recovery.
*   **Basic Anonymous Profile**: Handle + system avatar.
*   **Text-Based Anonymous Posts**: Create posts up to 500 characters.
*   **Post Categorization**: Assign posts to predefined categories.
*   **Anonymous Comments & Replies**: Threaded discussions.
*   **Anonymous Reactions**: Predefined emojis/reactions.
*   **Report Inappropriate Content**: User-driven moderation flagging.
*   **Personalized Anonymous Feed**: Basic interest-based content discovery.
*   **Trending Content Section**: Display popular posts.
*   **Category-Based Exploration**: Browse content by topic.
*   **AI Content Moderation**: Automated flagging and removal of harmful content.
*   **AI Sentiment Analysis**: Post-publication sentiment insights for authors.
*   **Moderation Queue for Admins**: Manual review of flagged content.
*   **Anonymous User Ban**: Ability to ban abusive users without revealing their identity.

**Timeline**: Q3 2026

### Phase 1: Core Enhancements & Engagement (v1.0)

**Goal**: Enhance user engagement, expand content types, and refine AI capabilities based on MVP feedback.

**Key Features**:
*   **End-to-End Encrypted Direct Messages**: Private one-on-one communication.
*   **AI-Generated Image/Audio Attachments**: Allow users to attach AI-modified media to posts.
*   **AI-Modified Media Labeling**: Clear labeling for all AI-generated/modified content.
*   **Advanced Search & Filters**: More granular control over content discovery.
*   **Bookmark/Save Posts**: Users can save content for later.
*   **Follow Anonymous Topics/Categories**: Subscribe to content streams.
*   **AI-Powered Content Summarization**: Summaries for long posts/threads.
*   **AI-Driven Content Tagging**: Automated tagging to improve discoverability.
*   **AI-Powered Spam Detection**: Enhanced protection against spam and bot activity.
*   **User Feedback & Reporting Tools**: Improved mechanisms for users to provide feedback and report issues.
*   **Community Guidelines & FAQ**: Comprehensive resources for users.

**Timeline**: Q4 2026 - Q1 2027

### Phase 2: Community & Personalization (v2.0)

**Goal**: Foster deeper community connections and offer more personalized experiences while maintaining anonymity.

**Key Features**:
*   **Anonymous Groups/Communities**: Users can join or create anonymous interest-based groups.
*   **Live Anonymous Chat Rooms**: Real-time anonymous discussions within groups or public channels.
*   **AI-Generated Post Suggestions**: Proactive content creation prompts for users.
*   **AI-Powered Persona Generation**: Users can generate anonymous personas with AI-created backstories/traits.
*   **Gamification Elements**: Anonymous badges, reputation scores (non-identifiable), leaderboards for engagement.
*   **Content Curation Tools**: Advanced tools for community moderators (anonymous).
*   **Multi-Language Support**: Expand platform to support multiple languages.
*   **Optional Premium Tier**: Introduce premium features (e.g., ad-free experience, advanced analytics for personal posts, more AI generations) while preserving core anonymity for all users.

**Timeline**: Q2 2027 - Q4 2027

### Phase 3: Platform Expansion & Ecosystem (v3.0)

**Goal**: Expand platform reach, integrate with external anonymous services, and build a self-sustaining ecosystem.

**Key Features**:
*   **API for Anonymous Integrations**: Allow third-party anonymous applications to integrate with Veil Shine (e.g., anonymous polling tools, anonymous Q&A sessions).
*   **Decentralized Storage Options**: Explore decentralized storage solutions for user content to enhance privacy and resilience.
*   **Advanced AI for Content Generation**: More sophisticated AI tools for users to create diverse anonymous content (e.g., short anonymous videos, interactive stories).
*   **AI-Powered Trend Prediction**: Identify emerging topics and sentiments before they become widespread.
*   **Educational Resources on Anonymity**: In-app guides and articles on digital privacy and anonymity best practices.
*   **Partnerships with Privacy Advocates**: Collaborate with organizations focused on digital rights and privacy.

**Timeline**: Q1 2028 onwards

## 3. Future Considerations (Beyond v3.0)

*   **Decentralized Identity Integration**: Explore integration with self-sovereign identity solutions.
*   **Advanced AI for Emotional Support**: AI-driven features to provide anonymous emotional support or guidance.
*   **VR/AR Anonymous Spaces**: Immersive anonymous social experiences.

## 4. Disclaimer

This roadmap is a living document and is subject to change. Priorities and timelines may be adjusted based on market conditions, technological advancements, user feedback, and business objectives. All features will adhere to the core principle of architectural anonymity and user privacy.
