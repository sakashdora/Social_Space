# Privacy Policy - Veil Shine

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Veil Shine! Our mission is to provide a truly anonymous social platform where you can express yourself freely and connect with others without the burden of personal identity. This Privacy Policy explains how Veil Shine (`"we"`, `"us"`, or `"our"`) handles information in a manner that upholds our core commitment to your anonymity and privacy. We are built on the principle of **"Anonymity by Architecture"**, meaning our systems are designed to make it impossible to link your activities to your real-world identity.

**We do not collect, store, or process Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from our users.**

This policy is designed to be transparent and to align with global privacy standards, including principles derived from the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), even though the nature of our service (no PII collection) inherently addresses many of their requirements.

## 2. Information We DO NOT Collect

To ensure your anonymity, Veil Shine **architecturally prevents the collection and storage** of the following Personally Identifiable Information (PII):

*   **Your Legal Name**
*   **Your Email Address**
*   **Your Phone Number**
*   **Your Date of Birth or ID Documents**
*   **Your Face, Voice, or Biometric Templates**
*   **Your Precise Location Data**
*   **Your Device Identifiers or Advertising IDs**
*   **A Graph of Who You Message (for direct messages, if implemented, we use sealed sender protocols)**
*   **Your Reading, Dwell-Time, or Scroll Behavior**
*   **Your IP Address (we use privacy-enhancing technologies to obscure or discard IP addresses)**

## 3. Information We DO Collect (Anonymous Data)

While we do not collect PII, we do process certain types of **anonymous data** that are essential for the functioning, security, and improvement of the Veil Shine platform. This data is inherently non-identifiable and cannot be linked back to you.

### 3.1. Anonymous User Identifiers
*   **User Handle**: A unique, self-chosen pseudonym that you use to interact on the platform. This is not linked to any real-world identity.
*   **Anonymous User ID (UUID)**: A system-generated Universally Unique Identifier that serves as your primary identifier within our system. This ID is randomly generated and contains no information that could identify you.

### 3.2. User-Generated Content
*   **Posts, Comments, Reactions**: The content you create and share on the platform. This content is associated with your anonymous User ID and handle, but never with your real identity.
*   **AI-Modified Media**: If you choose to use our AI features to generate or modify images or audio, this media is stored and associated with your anonymous post. We clearly label all AI-modified media.

### 3.3. Technical & Usage Data (Aggregated & Anonymized)
*   **Usage Statistics**: Aggregated and anonymized data about how the platform is used (e.g., number of posts created, most popular categories, feature usage). This data helps us understand overall trends and improve the service without identifying individuals.
*   **Error Logs**: Anonymous error reports and technical logs that help us diagnose and fix bugs. These logs do not contain any PII.
*   **Trust Tokens**: For abuse prevention, we may issue and verify privacy-preserving Trust Tokens. These tokens allow us to distinguish between legitimate users and malicious actors without revealing your identity, enabling us to ban bad behavior without knowing who you are.

## 4. How We Use Anonymous Information

The anonymous information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

*   **To Provide and Maintain the Service**: Operating the core functionalities of Veil Shine, including displaying your feed, processing your posts, comments, and reactions.
*   **To Ensure Anonymity and Security**: Implementing and maintaining the architectural safeguards that protect your identity and secure the platform against abuse.
*   **Content Moderation**: Utilizing AI to detect and remove harmful, offensive, or policy-violating content, ensuring a safe environment for all users. This process is based on the content itself, not on the identity of the poster.
*   **To Improve and Develop the Service**: Analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to understand how the platform is used and to develop new features and improvements.
*   **To Prevent Abuse**: Employing mechanisms like Trust Tokens to identify and mitigate malicious activity (e.g., spam, harassment) without compromising the anonymity of legitimate users.

## 5. How We Share Anonymous Information

We do not share any information that could identify you with third parties. All data we share is aggregated, anonymized, or pseudonymized to ensure it cannot be linked back to any individual user.

*   **Service Providers**: We may use third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, AI model providers) to assist in operating our platform. These providers are contractually bound to handle data in a manner consistent with our privacy principles and are prohibited from using anonymous data for any purpose other than providing services to Veil Shine.
*   **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose anonymous, aggregated data if required by law or in response to valid legal processes, but we will always prioritize protecting user anonymity and will challenge requests that seek to compromise it.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain anonymous user-generated content and associated anonymous metadata for as long as necessary to provide the service, enforce our terms, and comply with legal obligations. Given the anonymous nature of our platform, data retention policies are designed to balance service functionality with the principle of data minimization.

*   **User-Generated Content**: Posts and comments are retained on the platform to maintain the integrity of discussions and the historical context of the social network. If a user chooses to delete their anonymous account, their content will be disassociated from their anonymous User ID and handle, and may be retained in an even more anonymized form or removed based on our data retention policy.
*   **Moderation Logs**: Anonymous moderation logs are retained for auditing and to improve our moderation systems.

## 7. Your Rights (in an Anonymous Context)

While traditional privacy rights often revolve around access to and control over PII, Veil Shine adapts these rights to our anonymous model:

*   **Right to Access**: You can access the content you have created and associated anonymous metadata through your anonymous account.
*   **Right to Rectification**: You can edit or delete your own anonymous posts and comments.
*   **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: You can delete your anonymous account. Upon deletion, your anonymous User ID and handle will be permanently removed, and your content will be disassociated from any link to your past anonymous identity. The content itself may be retained in an aggregated, unlinked form for platform integrity and historical context, but it will no longer be attributable to your anonymous persona.
*   **Right to Object/Restrict Processing**: You can cease using the service if you object to our anonymous data processing practices.

To exercise these rights, please use the functionalities available within the Veil Shine application or contact our anonymous support channel.

## 8. Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures to protect the integrity and anonymity of our platform and your anonymous data. These measures include:

*   **Encryption**: All data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (database encryption, object storage encryption).
*   **Access Controls**: Strict internal access controls to our systems and databases.
*   **Regular Security Audits**: Periodic security assessments and penetration testing.
*   **Anonymity-Preserving Technologies**: Use of cryptographic techniques and architectural patterns to prevent re-identification.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our platform. Your continued use of Veil Shine after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our anonymous support channel within the Veil Shine application.
