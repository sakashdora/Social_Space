# Data Retention Policy - Veil Shine

## 1. Introduction

This Data Retention Policy outlines how Veil Shine manages the lifecycle of data generated and processed within its anonymous social platform. Our policy is fundamentally shaped by our commitment to **"Anonymity by Architecture"**, ensuring that data retention practices support user privacy and prevent the re-identification of individuals. We aim to retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain platform integrity, ensure security, and comply with legal obligations, all while respecting the anonymous nature of our user base.

## 2. Principles of Data Retention

*   **Data Minimization**: We only retain data that is essential for the functioning of the platform and for which there is a legitimate purpose.
*   **Anonymity Preservation**: All retained data is either inherently anonymous or is processed in a way that prevents its linkage to any real-world identity.
*   **Purpose Limitation**: Data is retained only for the specific purposes for which it was collected or generated.
*   **Security**: Retained data is protected with appropriate technical and organizational security measures.
*   **Transparency**: This policy clearly communicates our data retention practices to our users.
